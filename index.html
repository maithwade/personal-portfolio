Stripe Subscription Retention Analysis

Python SQL Looker Studio Pandas Seaborn

Cohort and churn analysis on 1,000 SaaS subscriptions, surfacing $15K+ MRR at risk and the strongest leading churn indicator.

4×churn disparity across tiers
93%of churned users had payment failure
$15K+MRR at risk identified
Problem

What drives churn across pricing tiers, and can payment failure patterns predict it before it happens?

Approach

Built a SQL and Pandas pipeline on subscription data, running cohort analysis and payment failure pattern mining using pandasql to isolate the strongest churn signals.

Finding

93% of churned customers had at least one payment failure, a stronger predictor than pricing tier or subscription length.

So What

Fixing payment recovery flows, not just pricing strategy, is the highest-leverage intervention to reduce churn and protect MRR.

Congestion Pricing Impact on NYC Rideshare Drivers

Python BigQuery Looker Studio Pandas Seaborn

End-to-end pipeline on 3M+ real NYC TLC trips, quantifying how congestion pricing shifted the earnings burden onto drivers.

3M+NYC TLC trips analyzed
45%more fare reduction absorbed by drivers
2quarters of policy impact tracked
Problem

NYC's congestion pricing policy reduced fares, but who actually absorbed the cost, passengers or drivers?

Approach

Engineered a Python and BigQuery pipeline on 3M+ TLC trip records, then ran borough and platform-level fare and compensation analysis across Q4 2024 and Q1 2025.

Finding

Drivers absorbed 45% more of the fare reduction than passengers, with the earnings gap widest in Manhattan and for Lyft drivers specifically.

So What

The policy created a structural earnings gap that disproportionately affected drivers, pointing to a need for fare floor protections in congestion pricing design.

Bluebikes Station Imbalance Analysis

Python Pandas Folium Matplotlib SciPy

Geospatial and statistical analysis on 1.6M+ Boston bike trips, identifying the 15 stations most at risk of peak-hour inventory depletion.

1.6M+Boston trip records
89%peak inventory depletion at risk stations
15high-risk stations flagged
Problem

Bluebikes stations run empty during morning commutes and overflow in evenings, but which stations need rebalancing most urgently?

Approach

Analyzed 1.6M+ trip records using Python and Pandas, engineering a netflow rate KPI across 594 stations and confirming commuter patterns via paired t-test.

Finding

15 stations showed up to 89% inventory depletion during peak hours, with statistically significant morning shortage and evening surplus patterns confirmed.

So What

A targeted rebalancing schedule for these 15 stations would address the majority of peak-hour supply gaps without overhauling the full network.
